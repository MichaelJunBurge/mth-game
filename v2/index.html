Reach the target, or work down to 0, using each of the six numbers once.

Tap a number (or the target) to add it to your line, then a math symbol, then another number.

Each new number you make becomes a chip you can reuse.

c: tap to undo a step, or hold it down to keep deleting. Press = to bank the line and start fresh.

Auto (=) (menu) solves each step as you go; turn it off to build a full sum and press =.

Auto sign (menu) picks which symbol drops in when you tap two numbers in a row.

Swipe (or use the side arrows) to switch calculators — swipe past the last to add one — and the sketchpad on the far left. Dots show where you are. Press and hold the calculator to reveal the × that deletes the page.

Submit is in this menu (tap the timer). It scores the page you're on and goes green once all six numbers are used.
